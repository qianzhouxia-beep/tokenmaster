Frequently Asked Questions

How does TokenMaster save me 75% vs OpenAI? expand_more
We aggregate demand across thousands of indie developers and route to the cheapest available provider (Zhipu GLM-4-Plus, DeepSeek, Qwen) at 25% of OpenAI's price. The same OpenAI-compatible API, the same model behavior — 75% off the bill.
Which AI models can I access? expand_more
18+ models live: Zhipu GLM-4-Plus (with 24M+ free tokens for the Indie tier and above), DeepSeek-V3, Qwen-Max, GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Gemini Pro 1.5, Mistral Large, Llama 3 70B, and more — all under one OpenAI-compatible endpoint.
Do I need to provide my own API key? expand_more
No. Buy tokens on TokenMaster and you get one master key. Pass it as the `Authorization: Bearer` header and you have access to all 18+ models. No separate OpenAI / Anthropic / Zhipu accounts needed.
How do payments work? expand_more
Two options: (1) PayPal —credit card, debit, PayPal balance, all major currencies. (2) NOWPayments —crypto, including USDT, USDC, BTC, ETH on Ethereum / TRON / Polygon. Payment confirms in seconds, tokens are issued to your account instantly. No subscription, no auto-debit.
Can I get a refund? expand_more
Yes. Unused tokens are refundable within 14 days of purchase, no questions asked. Contact support@api-tokenmaster.com with your transaction ID. Used tokens are non-refundable, but your remaining balance carries over (see below).
What happens when my tokens run out or expire? expand_more
When tokens run out, your API key returns 402 Payment Required — buy more and resume. Tokens are valid for 12 months from purchase date; any unused balance auto-renews for another 12 months with a 10% bonus credit (90% roll-over policy).
Is my data secure? expand_more
Yes. All requests are TLS-encrypted end-to-end. We do not log prompt or completion content. We do not train on your data. Payment data goes to PayPal / NOWPayments — we never see your card or wallet. IP whitelisting is available on Pro and above.
How do I get started? expand_more
Three steps. (1) Pick a plan above and pay via PayPal or crypto. (2) Get your master API key instantly via email. (3) Point your OpenAI SDK to https://api-tokenmaster.com/v1 with the new key — that's it. Detailed setup guide in our docs after launch.
