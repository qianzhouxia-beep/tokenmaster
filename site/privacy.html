Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

1. What we collect

When you buy tokens on TokenMaster, we collect:

  • Email address — to deliver your API key and send service notifications.
  • Payment metadata — transaction IDs, amounts, currency, timestamp. We do not see your card or wallet; payments are processed by PayPal or NOWPayments.
  • Account usage — number of API requests, tokens consumed, error rates. Used for billing and capacity planning.

When you use the API, we collect:

  • HTTP request metadata — your IP, request timestamp, model id, token counts, response status. Used for rate limiting, abuse prevention, and billing.
  • We do not log your prompts or completions. The content you send through the API is not stored, reviewed, or used to train any model.

2. How we use your data

We use collected data only to operate the service: route your requests, bill you accurately, prevent abuse, and notify you of security incidents. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except:

  • With the upstream AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Zhipu, DeepSeek, Qwen, Mistral, Meta) when routing your API requests. Each provider has their own privacy policy.
  • With payment processors (PayPal, NOWPayments) to handle your payment.
  • If required by law, court order, or to prevent fraud or abuse.

3. Cookies & local storage

We use a single piece of data in your browser: tm_lang, which remembers your language preference (English or Chinese). We do not use third-party tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising pixels on the landing or documentation pages.

4. Data retention

  • Account & billing data: retained for 7 years to comply with tax and accounting regulations.
  • API request metadata: aggregated logs retained for 90 days for abuse investigation, then deleted.
  • Prompt/completion content: not stored. Period.

5. Your rights

You can request to export or delete your account data at any time by emailing support@api-tokenmaster.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Security

All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3. All data at rest is encrypted with AES-256. API keys are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plaintext. We use cryptographic webhook signatures (PayPal Webhook ID + NOWPayments HMAC-SHA512) to prevent request forgery.

7. Contact

Privacy questions: support@api-tokenmaster.com. See also our Contact page.
