Our mission

AI is becoming the operating system of the next decade of software. But the price of leading models (GPT-4, Claude 3.5) puts a 10x–20x markup on top of what they actually cost to serve. That gap is held back by market structure: each provider runs its own API, its own pricing, its own rate limits. As an indie developer shipping a small product, you have to choose: pay the markup, or run a fragile multi-account setup.

TokenMaster aggregates demand across thousands of indie developers and routes to whichever upstream provider is cheapest at the moment. We pass those savings through. You get one master key, one bill, one dashboard — and the same OpenAI-compatible API you already know.

What we believe

  • Open standards win. We're OpenAI-compatible on purpose. You can switch providers without rewriting your code.
  • Privacy is non-negotiable. We never log your prompts or completions. We never train on your data.
  • Pay only for what you use. No monthly minimums, no seat fees, no auto-debit. Buy tokens, use them, top up when you need more.
  • Honest pricing. The numbers on the pricing page are the numbers you pay. No surprise overage charges.

Who we are

TokenMaster is a small team of engineers and product folks building AI infrastructure. We're based across time zones (US, Europe, Asia) so support is fast and uptime is high. We've been running production AI workloads since 2024 and have shipped infrastructure used by indie developers, agencies, and small startups across 30+ countries.
